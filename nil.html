About Ava

A gymnast with a stage — and an audience that shows up.

I grew up in Lehi chalking my hands before I could spell my name. Now I compete in front of one of the largest, loudest crowds in the sport and share every step — training, meets, recovery, and real life — with a community that trusts what I put in front of them.

For brands, that means genuine reach: young athletes, gymnastics families, and Utah fans who buy what I stand behind.
