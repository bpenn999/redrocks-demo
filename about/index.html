About

Meet Christian Brindle

Warm, plain-spoken, patient, and genuinely helpful. Explains Medicare like a trusted neighbor, never a salesperson. No jargon, no pressure.

Christian Brindle is an independent, Salt Lake City, Utah–based founder & licensed insurance advisor focused on Medicare and senior insurance. Independent means Christian isn't tied to one insurance company — so the guidance you get is based on your doctors, your prescriptions, and your budget, not a single carrier's lineup.

How we work

We keep it human. You'll get a real person who explains your options in plain English, checks that your doctors and medications are covered before you enroll, and reviews your plan every fall so it keeps fitting as plans change.

What we help with

  • Medicare Advantage (Part C) — All-in-one plans that bundle hospital, medical, and usually drug coverage — often with $0 premiums and extras like dental, vision, and hearing.
  • Medicare Supplement (Medigap) — Standardized plans that pay the gaps in Original Medicare so you can see any doctor that accepts Medicare, nationwide, with very predictable costs.
  • Part D Prescription Drug Plans — Stand-alone drug coverage that keeps your prescriptions affordable — now with a $2,000 yearly out-of-pocket cap.
  • ACA Health Insurance (Under 65) — Affordable Care Act Marketplace plans for Utahns who aren't yet on Medicare — with premium tax credits that lower what you pay each month.

Our promise

No pressure, no jargon, and no cost to compare. If a plan isn't right for you, we'll say so.
