Good to Know

Does my child need prior experience? +
No. The Little Utes and All-Levels sessions are built for beginners through intermediate gymnasts. The Elite Prep Clinic is designed for Level 7+ competitors.
Will the actual team be there? +
Yes. Sessions are led by Red Rocks coaching staff, and current student-athletes join each day to coach, demo, and take photos with campers.
What should my camper bring? +
A leotard or athletic clothes, hair tie, water bottle, and grips if they use them. Everything else — equipment, lunch on full days, and camp gear — is provided.
What's the refund policy? +
Full refund up to 14 days before the session start date. Within 14 days, we'll credit your registration toward any other 2026 session.
How do I know my spot is confirmed? +
You'll get a confirmation text and email within minutes of registering, followed by a camper info packet the week before your session.
