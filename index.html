Common questions

Straight answers

How much does it cost to work with you?

Nothing. Like most independent Medicare advisors, we're compensated by the insurance carriers when you enroll — so comparing plans and getting our help costs you the same as going direct, which is $0. We do not offer every plan available in your area.

Do you offer every Medicare plan?

No. We do not offer every plan available in your area. Currently we represent 76 organizations which offer 2,345 plans nationwide. For all of your options, contact Medicare.gov, 1-800-MEDICARE, or your State Health Insurance Program (SHIP).

Are you part of Medicare or the government?

No. Christian Brindle Insurance Services is not connected with or endorsed by the U.S. government or the federal Medicare program. We're an independent, licensed insurance agency that helps you compare private plans.

When should I start looking at Medicare?

About 3 months before you turn 65 — that's when your Initial Enrollment Period opens. If you're already on Medicare, the fall Annual Enrollment Period (Oct 15 – Dec 7) is the time to review your plan for the coming year.

I already have a plan. Can you review it?

Absolutely. Plans change their premiums, drug lists, and networks every year. We do a free annual review to make sure your current plan still fits your doctors, medications, and budget — and only suggest a change if it genuinely helps you.
